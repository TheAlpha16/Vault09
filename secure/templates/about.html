Welcome to Vault09!
Vault09 is an application designed to provide secure and reliable file storage for users seeking to protect their valuable data. With Vault09, your files remain completely secure, even in the event of a system compromise.
At Vault09, we understand the importance of data privacy. That's why we utilize the Advanced Encryption Standard, known for its exceptional security features.
We believe in providing a seamless and hassle-free user experience. Vault09 operates locally on your machine, leveraging Docker to ensure efficient and reliable performance.
Protecting your data is our top priority. Vault09 adheres to a strict security protocol, ensuring that your encryption passwords and hashes are never stored. By adopting this approach, we eliminate any potential risks of unauthorized access, guaranteeing that only you can decrypt your data.
Vault09 embraces diversity by supporting encryption for all file types. Whether it's documents, images, videos, or any other file format, you can rely on Vault09 to encrypt and secure your files effectively.
